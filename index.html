About Our Company

With decades of experience in steel manufacturing, JAINAM FERRO ALLOYS stands as a leading manufacturer of high-quality stainless steel, carbon steel, and alloy steel products. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted partner for industries worldwide.

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we maintain the highest standards of quality in our cold drawn pipes and tubes processing. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced team ensure precision engineering and reliable delivery for all your steel requirements.

We specialize in custom solutions, working closely with our clients to deliver products that meet their exact specifications and industry standards.
